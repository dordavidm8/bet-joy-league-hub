## Style Prompt
Dark, high-energy sports betting app explainer. Slate-black canvas with electric green accents pulled from a football pitch. Typography pairs a heavy condensed display face (muscle, speed, sport) against a monospace data face (odds, scores, precision). Motion is fast and confident — this is a promo, not a tutorial.

## Colors
- `#0f172a` — background (slate-900, near-black)
- `#f8fafc` — foreground text (slate-50)
- `#22c55e` — primary accent (green-500, pitch green)
- `#1e293b` — card surface (slate-800)
- `#64748b` — muted text (slate-500)
- `#ef4444` — live/alert red

## Typography
- **Barlow Condensed 900** — all display headlines (logo, CTA, team names). Tall, compressed, athletic.
- **DM Mono 400/700** — all data: scores, odds, points, labels. Fixed-width precision.
- Pairing tension: human kinetic energy vs machine readout.

## What NOT to Do
- No cyan, purple, or neon gradients — this is pitch green, not gaming LED
- No gradient text (background-clip: text) — it's an AI cliché
- No left-edge accent stripes on cards
- No centered layout for every scene — use zone-based splits for data scenes
- No identical card grids — each scene has its own compositional logic
